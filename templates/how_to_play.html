📖 How to Play

In this game, you and your partner will annotate sentences that express geopolitical risks (GPR). Your goal is to agree on which sentences to select from a paragraph.

🔧 Instructions

  • Click on sentences you believe contain geopolitical risk signals.
  • Click “✅ Confirm Selection” when you're done.
  • You'll be matched with your partner's selections.
  • If your selections match, you both get points!
  • You have up to 3 attempts per paragraph to reach agreement.

💡 Tips

  • Only match on substantive risks — avoid highlighting trivial or unrelated sentences.
  • Be thoughtful: longer sentences or vague expressions may not count.
  • Speed matters: quicker agreement means higher score!

Ready? Return to your game window and begin!
