Geopolitical risk refers to the threat, realization, or escalation of adverse events related to wars or terrorism that affect international relations.

üîÑ GPR Events and Stages

GPR sentences may describe different stages:

  • Threat: Early warnings or risks that war or terrorism may happen
  • Realization: Actual war/attack has occurred
  • Escalation: Worsening or spread of ongoing conflict

GPR = Event Type (War / Terrorism) + Stage (Threat / Realization / Escalation)
